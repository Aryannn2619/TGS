Diet Strategies

Pick the framework that fits your goal & life.

Bulking

+250–500 kcal surplus. Maximize muscle growth without excessive fat gain.

Cutting

−400–600 kcal deficit. Lose fat, retain muscle. Increase protein.

Maintenance

Hold the line. Recomp at TDEE — slow but sustainable changes.

Intermittent Fasting

16:8 / 18:6 protocols. Insulin sensitivity boost, simpler eating.

Keto / Low Carb

<50g carbs/day. Fat-adapted energy. 70% fat / 25% protein / 5% carbs.

Vegan / Plant-based

100% plants. Combine sources for full amino profile. B12 + creatine non-negotiable.

📸 The Plate Method

A visual cheat-code: assemble your meal by zones, not by counting.

PROTEIN ZONE

1/4 Plate · Lean Protein

Palm-sized portion (~150–200g cooked). Chicken, fish, paneer, tofu, eggs.

CARB ZONE

1/4 Plate · Smart Carbs

Cupped-hand serving. Rice, oats, sweet potato, quinoa, whole-wheat roti.

FIBRE ZONE

1/2 Plate · Vegetables

Two fistfuls minimum. Leafy greens, cruciferous, colored peppers. Volume hack.

The 5 Habits That Outweigh Everything Else

  1. Track everything for 7 days. Awareness is the first lever. Most people undercount by 30%.
  2. Hit your protein floor. 1.6–2.2g per kg bodyweight. Spread across 3–5 meals.
  3. Pre-log meals. Decide ahead — willpower fails when hungry.
  4. Whole foods 80% of the time. 80/20 rule. Restrictive diets fail; flexible ones win.
  5. Review weekly. Adjust based on the scale + mirror + lifts. Small tweaks, not crashes.
