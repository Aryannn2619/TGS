🧬 The 4 Sleep Stages

Each stage has a job. Disrupt one, lose the gains.

N1

5% of night · 5–10 min · brain & body slow down. Easy to wake.

N2

Light Sleep

45–55% · memory consolidation, motor learning, body temp drops.

N3

Deep Sleep

15–25% · physical recovery, GH peak, immune repair. THE anabolic stage.

REM

REM Sleep

20–25% · dreams, brain detox, memory & mood. Alcohol kills it.
