📸 Progress Photo Protocol

Done right, photos are the most accurate progress signal.

  1. Same time of day. First thing in the morning, post-toilet, pre-water.
  2. Same lighting. Natural daylight or same indoor light. No filters.
  3. Same outfit. Tight shorts/briefs. Women: sports bra + shorts. Same pose, no flexing on day 1, slight flex same way each time.
  4. Same poses. Front · side · back · front-flexed. Phone at chest height, 2m away.
  5. Same background. Plain wall. Reduces visual noise.
  6. Compare every 4 weeks. Side-by-side. Daily comparisons drive you crazy.
