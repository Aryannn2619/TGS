📚 The Habit Lab

Each habit, why it matters, and the protocol to install it.

💧 Hydration

Daily · Multiplier

Formula: 35 ml/kg bodyweight + 500 ml per hour of training. Pee should be straw-colored. Install: 500ml on wake. Keep a 1L bottle visible. Add electrolytes if >90 min training.

👟 10,000 Steps

Daily · NEAT

Why: Boosts non-exercise calorie burn (NEAT) by 300–500 kcal/day. Mood, BP, blood sugar. Install: 10-min walks after meals. Walking calls. Park far. Take stairs.

🪑 Posture

Hourly

Best fix: Daily T-spine extensions, wall slides, face pulls 3×/week. Install: Stand-up alarm every hour. Standing desk. Phone at eye level. Pull-up bar in doorway.

🌪️ Stress Management

Daily

Tools: Box breathing, 120 min/wk in nature, social connection, news fast. Install: Schedule worry-free hours. Do hard thing first. Say no more often.

🚫 Alcohol Reduction

Critical

Cost: Each drink kills 90 min REM, lowers protein synthesis 24%, raises cortisol. Install: Limit 2/week. Order non-alcoholic first. Keep zero in fridge.

📵 Digital Hygiene

Daily

Why: Phone in 1st 30 min wrecks dopamine baseline. Phone in last 60 min wrecks REM. Install: Phone outside bedroom. Notifications off. Greyscale mode after 8 PM.

☀️ Morning Sunlight

Daily

Why: Sets cortisol peak + melatonin onset 14h later. Install: Coffee outdoors. 10-min walk. No sunglasses (don't stare at sun).

🍎 Whole Food First

Per meal

Rule: Eat protein + veg before anything processed. Install: Fruit visible, junk hidden. 80/20 rule. Cook 5 nights/week.

🧠 The Atomic Habits Framework

James Clear's 4 laws · Use these to install or break any habit.

1. Make it Obvious

Cue visible. Habit-stack: "After I [current habit], I will [new habit]."

2. Make it Attractive

Pair with something you enjoy. Join a community doing the same.

3. Make it Easy

2-minute rule. Reduce friction to start. Prep gear the night before.

4. Make it Satisfying

Track streaks. Visual chain. Celebrate small wins immediately.
