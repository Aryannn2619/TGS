🧠 5 Mindset Frameworks

Tools the elites use. Adopt one per week.

1. Stoicism · Dichotomy of Control

Control your effort, attitude, response. Surrender outcomes. Anxiety lives where you violate this.

2. Growth Mindset · Carol Dweck

Add "yet" to every failure. "I can't do pull-ups" → "I can't do pull-ups yet." Reframes identity.

3. Identity-based Habits · James Clear

"I'm a runner" > "I want to run." Every action is a vote for who you're becoming.

4. The 1-2 Rule · David Goggins

When you want to quit, you're at 40%. The 1-2 rule: do one more rep, take two more breaths.

5. Cognitive Reframing · CBT

Spot the distortion: catastrophizing, all-or-nothing, mind-reading. Name it. Replace it.

6. Memento Mori

Death is the ultimate clarifier. "Will this matter in 5 years?" cuts 90% of stress.
