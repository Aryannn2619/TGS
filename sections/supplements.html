⚠️ Safety Protocol

  • Get blood work first. Vitamin D, ferritin, B12, lipids, hormones. You can't supplement what you don't measure.
  • Buy third-party tested. Look for NSF Certified for Sport, Informed Sport, USP, or LabDoor stamps.
  • Pregnant, on meds, under 18? Doctor first, supplement second. Always.
  • More is not better. Megadosing fat-soluble vitamins (A, D, E, K) is toxic.
  • Whole food > pills. No supplement beats real protein, vegetables, and sleep.
  • Cycle stimulants. Caffeine tolerance builds in 7–10 days. Pre-workouts: max 5 days/week.
